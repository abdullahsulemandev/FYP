About Us

Welcome to SilverScreenIQ, your trusted partner in creating safe and enjoyable viewing experiences for everyone.

Our platform is designed to empower users with advanced tools to analyze and edit videos, ensuring content aligns with personal, family, or community standards. Whether you're a parent safeguarding what your family watches or a professional preparing content for a broader audience, our solution seamlessly removes inappropriate scenes such as nudity, kissing, and violence with precision and ease.

Privacy Policy

At SilverScreenIQ, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our platform. By accessing our services, you agree to the practices below.

We collect uploaded videos temporarily for processing, and they are automatically deleted within 2 hours after completion. If you create an account, we collect your name and email address, but no sensitive data is collected unless required.

Your information is used to process and censor uploaded videos, provide processed videos with timestamps, improve website functionality, and respond to your inquiries. We do not sell, rent, or share your data for marketing purposes.

Uploaded videos are stored only for processing and are automatically deleted after 2 hours. We do not retain or access video content beyond the analysis phase, ensuring strict privacy.

We prioritize data security with encryption, secure servers, and regular audits. However, as no method of transmission or storage is entirely secure, we recommend caution when sharing sensitive information.

Cookies are used to analyze traffic, remember preferences, and improve functionality. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, though disabling cookies may affect some website features.

We use trusted third-party services (e.g., cloud storage providers) to support our platform securely and responsibly.

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data, request information on how your data is processed, and opt out of non-essential data collection. To exercise these rights, contact us at silverscreeniq@gmail.com.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with the updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of content does your platform detect and censor?

Our platform is designed to detect and censor inappropriate content, including nudity, kissing scenes, and violence. These categories help ensure the video aligns with family-friendly or professional standards.

2. How accurate is the content detection?

Our system uses advanced AI models trained on diverse datasets to ensure high accuracy in detecting inappropriate content. While no system is perfect, we constantly update our algorithms to improve precision and minimize errors.

3. Can I customize what gets censored?

Yes! You can choose which categories to censor. For example, you can opt to remove only nudity or violence, depending on your preferences.

4. Will the edited video lose quality?

No, the edited video retains the original quality as much as possible. Our platform ensures that only the inappropriate segments are removed, while the rest of the video remains untouched.

5. How long does it take to analyze and render a video?

The time required depends on the length and size of the video. Typically, shorter videos are processed in just a few minutes, while longer ones may take a bit more time.

6. Is my uploaded video safe?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our priority. All uploaded videos are processed securely and are not stored on our servers once the analysis and editing are complete.

7. Can I download the timestamps of detected scenes?

Yes, along with the edited video, you’ll receive a detailed report listing the timestamps of all detected inappropriate scenes for your reference.
